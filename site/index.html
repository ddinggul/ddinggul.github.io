Research 연구

Questions I am working on. 지금 탐구 중인 질문들.

Can AI perceive the melody and rhythm of human speech?

Suprasegmental features — prosody, stress, and intonation — carry meaning that transcends individual phonemes. I am interested in whether and how AI systems can learn to recognise these patterns the way human listeners do.

Where do native and non-native speech patterns diverge?

L1–L2 contrastive analysis using mel-spectrograms and TextGrid-based alignment reveals fine-grained prosodic differences. Contrastive learning approaches help surface what distinguishes learner speech from native norms.

How can language models support real language learning?

From fine-tuned grammar correction pipelines to Vogler-structured narrative modelling and vocabulary systems grounded in Suggestopedia, I explore practical NLP applications that go beyond benchmark scores.
