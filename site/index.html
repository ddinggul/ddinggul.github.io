How does speech carry meaning beyond words?

Prosody shapes emphasis, grouping, affect, and stance. I am interested in making these patterns measurable without losing their linguistic nuance.

Where does second-language speech converge or diverge?

L1-L2 comparison can reveal subtle differences in timing, pitch movement, and stress. These differences are useful for both theory and learner feedback.

What should language-learning AI actually help with?

I care about feedback that is contextual, specific, and usable: not just scores, but explanations that help learners notice patterns.
